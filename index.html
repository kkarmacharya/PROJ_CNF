About Us

Welcome to
Changunarayan Foundation

Changunarayan Foundation is a registered non-profit organization under the Government of Nepal, dedicated to improving the lives of Nepali citizens. Our core purpose is to conduct meaningful social services that uplift communities, promote sustainable development, and create a brighter future for all Nepalese.

Changunarayan Foundation was born out of a deep commitment to serve the people of Nepal. Over the years, we have worked tirelessly to address critical issues such as poverty, healthcare access, disaster relief, and education. Through our various initiatives, we aim to bring positive change to vulnerable communities, empowering individuals to build a better future.

Our Initiatives


Empowering
individuals to build a better future.

Vocational Training

Changunarayan Foundation provides hands-on training in different sectors, and digital literacy to empower disadvantaged individuals with skills for employment and self-sufficiency.

Healthcare Access

We believe that access to healthcare is a fundamental right. Through mobile clinics, partnerships with health centers, and awareness campaigns, we work to improve healthcare access for rural communities.

Education for All

Education is the cornerstone of development. We provide scholarships, educational materials, and support to schools in remote areas. Our aim is to ensure that every Nepali child has access to quality education, regardless of their background.

Empowerment for All

Changunarayan Foundation is dedicated to uplifting women, children, and marginalized communities through education, skill development, and awareness programs for equality and self-reliance.

Sustainable Development

We are dedicated to environmental sustainability. Our initiatives focus on promoting eco-friendly practices, including tree planting programs and waste management systems that contribute to the protection of our natural resources.
